When Was The Areyto Chapter Established?

The Areyto Chapter was founded on November 28th, 1993 at New Jersey City University. It is the 9th chapter of Lambda Sigma Upsilon Latino Fraternity, Inc

Our Mission

To uplift and unite underrepresented communities by empowering tomorrow's leaders.

Our 4 Core Values

  1. Contribute to and promote academic excellence among the brothers in pursuit of a higher education degree.
  2. Promote cultural awareness as well as cultural diversity.
  3. Be role models to students of higher education institutions and the community at large.
  4. An eternal brotherhood where men from all walks of life are supported by one another.
